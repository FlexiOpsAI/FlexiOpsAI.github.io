Attach-Rate: bundle where it counts

Fridays • 3–6pm • Pastrami orders
+12% est. tickets with chips
Action → Offer “Pastrami + Chips” at checkout; prompt staff; signage at counter.

Reasoning: historical lift on similar bundles (+9–15%); strong foot-traffic window; low prep overhead.

Weather impact: adjust menu focus

This Fri • Rain forecast 70%
Hot sandwiches ↑ ~10% · Cold ↓ ~20%
Action → Feature hot items in displays; throttle prep on cold SKUs; adjust labor for grill line.

Reasoning: rain-day patterns last season; similar Fridays; local event calendar.

Prep-List: right-size for the rush

Sat • 10:30am target • Lunch rush
Prep 38 ciabatta rolls · 24 chicken trays
Action → Stage by 10:20am; re-check at 12:15pm; hold 15% buffer.

Reasoning: trailing 8 Saturdays, weather + promo lift, seasonality.
