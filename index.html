📦 Ingredient Forecast

"Know exactly what to prep before the rush hits"

The Problem: You either run out of ingredients during busy times (losing sales), or you prep too much and throw food away at closing (losing money).

How It Works: Our AI analyzes your past sales patterns, day of the week, and local events to predict exactly what you'll need.

Example — a Saturday prep plan:
"Based on your last 4 Saturdays, prep:
• 50 pastrami portions (up from ~38)
• 35 turkey portions (down from ~45)
• 65 bread loaves (up from ~52)"

🥪 Catering-Aware Prep Live

"Catch catering before it walks out the door"

The Problem: A big catering order lands and the prep wasn't planned for it — or an off-POS order (like ezCater) never shows up in your numbers at all.

How It Works: Known future catering orders are folded into your weekly prep automatically, and off-POS catering is reconciled back against your sales — so nothing is a surprise and nothing is missed.

Example:
"Thursday has a 30-box-lunch order booked for 11am. Your prep plan already includes it — with a printable make/pull sheet for the team."
Sample figures, for illustration.

Why it's different: Most tools only see what your POS sees. This one also sees your off-POS catering — the orders that usually slip through.
